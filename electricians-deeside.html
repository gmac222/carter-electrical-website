Frequently asked questions

Are your electricians NICEIC-approved and qualified to work in Deeside?

Yes. Carter Electrical Contracting is a fully NICEIC-approved contractor, and every electrician dispatched to Deeside works to BS 7671 (18th Edition, including the latest amendments). Certification is issued at handover on every job — EIC, minor works or EICR as appropriate — and logged against the property address so it's straightforward to retrieve if you need it for insurance, a landlord pack or a future sale.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency call-out in Deeside?

From our Christleton base we typically reach Deeside in 45–75 minutes for genuine emergencies — loss of power, a burning-plastic smell, water ingress near a consumer unit, or tripped main breakers that won't reset. Our phone line is answered during working hours by the same team that schedules the engineers, so you'll get an honest answer on timings rather than a call-handler promise.

Which Deeside postcodes and neighbourhoods do you cover?

We cover every postcode inside Deeside and its surrounding villages — primarily CH5. That includes Shotton, Connah's Quay, Queensferry, Sealand, Hawarden and the rest of the Flintshire (Wales) area. If you're unsure whether your address is in our usual radius, call us with the postcode and we'll confirm before scheduling.

Do you carry out EICRs and landlord electrical-safety reports in Deeside?

Yes. We're a go-to contractor for EICRs across Deeside — both for commercial landlords ahead of lease renewals and for residential landlords meeting the five-yearly Electrical Safety Standards in the Private Rented Sector (England) Regulations 2020, or the equivalent Welsh legislation where applicable. Reports are issued as a signed PDF within 48 hours of the test, with remedial quotes broken out clearly so you can decide what's urgent.

Can you install an OZEV-approved EV charger at a home or business in Deeside?

Yes — we're OZEV-approved and install 7 kW single-phase and 22 kW three-phase chargers throughout Deeside. For domestic installs we handle the DNO notification, load management (so your main supply doesn't get overloaded) and OLEV/EV-chargepoint grant paperwork where you're eligible. For workplace installs we can scope multi-bay back-office systems with RFID authorisation and billing integration.

Do you work on heritage, listed or conservation-area properties in Deeside?

Yes, and we do it often. Deeside's older property stock needs a different hand — surface-clipped cable runs on stone walls, concealed containment through plasterwork, period-sympathetic switch and socket plates, and documentation that will satisfy both a conservation officer and the NICEIC. We scope heritage work slowly on the first visit so you know exactly what's going where before anything is cut or chased.
